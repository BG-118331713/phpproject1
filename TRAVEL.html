Travel



From a young age I have always been fascinated by all the different cultures around the world and how each culture varies from the next. I am fortunate enough to have been able to travel to many countries in the world already. I have journeyed to many different countries in Europe such as France, England, Italy, Portugal, Spain, Czech Republic, Germany and Austria. I thoroughly enjoyed travelling around Europe, trying different countries cuisine and meeting new people along the way. However, I found my trips to countries outside Europe, which were a bit further from home, the most exciting. I enjoyed my visit to Washington D.C. and New York in America but my adventure to Egypt was my most eye opening trip yet, it was a truly amazing experience.
